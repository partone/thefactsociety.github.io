The Fact Society beckons you forth to begin your journey towards truly understanding knowledge. The Fact Society is composed of a small group of investigators who seek to explore and analyse the world around us. We understand that frequently data is confusing, evidence is contradictory, and the truth is hard to pin; therefore we have given ourselves the task of selecting the most controversial topics and placing them under a microscope to identify the facts beyond the facts.
