Our Offering
We offer our clients direct mitochondria transfusions from pure ethnic Sherpas.

This one-of-a-kind procedure has the long-lasting effect of improving the recipient's ability to handle low oxygen environments. As a direct consequence, recipients can endure high altitudes better, reduce muscle strain during exercise, and improve their muscle growth.

This is possible due to a unique evolution in Sherpa mitochondrial DNA (mtDNA) that has resulted in a better adaptation to their environment high in the Himalayas.

Steps-by-Step Process
Contact Us
Prior to your transfusion
1. Meet with us in our San Diego office.
We will happily discuss your medical history, any possible risks involved in mitochondria transfusions, and share past client testimonials.
2. Schedule a date for your operation.
3. Allow us to handle the rest.
Everbest™ will arrange your immigration requirements for Mexico travel and organize air transportation for a Nepalese Sherpa ahead of your transfusion day.
The day of your transfusion
1. On the day of your transfusion, arrive at our San Diego office at 7am.
You will receive confirmation of your Sherpa donor's arrival to our Tijuana facility 24 hours prior to your transfusion.
2. Our private chauffeur will taxi you to our secure medical facility in Tijuana.
3. Your mitochondria transfusion will commence around 9am and last approximately two hours.
Feel free to bring headphones and music. We also offer complimentary Wifi, snacks, and foot massages within the facility.

After the procedure, we kindly ask that you remain in the facility for two more hours for monitoring by our trained staff.
4. Our chauffeur will taxi you back to our office in San Diego for after-care instructions.
After your transfusion
1. You will take a course of immunosuppressant for four weeks while your body adjusts to its new mitochondria.
2. You will feel the first improvements to cardiovascular endurance within the first two to four weeks
Your new mitochondria will need time to replicate and displace your old mitochondria.
3. After 40 weeks the procedure will be complete - cheers to living your Everbest™ life.
